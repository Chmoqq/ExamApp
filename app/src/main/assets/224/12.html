RSPB Minsmere Nature Reserve

There are several miles of countryside walks through Suffolk’s gorgeous coastal and wetland scenery, bringing you close to a wide variety of wildlife. Families and youth groups can explore the Wild Zone and Wild Wood Adventure, discovering more about how some of Minsmere’s special wildlife lives whilst having fun.

Adult £7.50, Child (4-18) £3, RSPB members & RSPB Wildlife Explorers FREE. Nature trails and hikes open daily dawn-dusk.
